The Unusual Death of Mrs Morris is a retro science fiction tale about dealing with societal change and broken technology. Mrs Morris arrives home with a bag full of groceries and finds her refrigerator containing a corpse. But the strangest part is that the corpse appears to be her own.

Excerpt: 

"On the day Mrs Morris found the body crammed into her refrigerator she had just returned from the grocery store. Just a week earlier she had lodged a complaint with the company that manufactured the device. The SchrankSystem® allowed remote delivery of her groceries via a micro-wormhole..."
